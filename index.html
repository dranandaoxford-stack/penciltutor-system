Penciltutor — Strip #001

Observation vs Assumption

Panel 1
A wilted plant sits by the classroom window.
Alex: “The plant is dead.”
Panel 2
Sam looks closer.
Sam: “That’s an assumption. What do we actually observe?”
Panel 3
Alex: “The leaves are drooping… and the soil looks dry.”
Panel 4
They water the plant.
Sam: “Let’s test before concluding.”
Panel 5
The plant recovers.
Alex: “So it wasn’t dead — just thirsty.”
Panel 6
Rule: In science, observe first. Conclusions come later.
